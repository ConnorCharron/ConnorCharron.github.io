How it works

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity for your home

Solar panels contain photovoltaic cells that absorb sunlight and transform it into usable power. Most homes use monocrystalline panels for efficiency, or polycrystalline panels for affordability. An inverter converts DC power to AC power for your appliances. Atlantic Climate Control handles installation, permits, and maintenance so you don't have to.
